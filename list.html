Recipe ko kase luluto ako tofu sisig

Ingrdients

Dried Ingredients:

  • tofu
  • garlic
  • onion
  • chili red and green
  • margarine or butter

Para sa Ingredients na sauce:

  • mayonnaise
  • oyster sauce
  • ground pepper
  • sugar
  • calamansi

Instructions

  1. prito mo tokwa
  2. painitin mo pan
  3. lagay mo margarine pwede ka magmantika para di masunog
  4. lagay mo na sibuyas at bawang
  5. lagay mo na green chila and red chili
  6. lagay mo na tokwa
  7. lagay mo na sauce
  8. haluhin and lagyan egg
  9. serve mo na sa shota mo
